PRIVACY
As an organisation, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We take all reasonable measures, in accordance with legislation and this policy, to protect personal information which is collected, possessed, and retained when you use our website.

WHAT IS PERSONAL INFORMATION?
Personal information refers to information that identifies or relates specifically to you, for example, your name, age, race, gender, and identity number. Any information about what you buy, where you bank, and related information will also be regarded as personal information. To summarize, any information that we know about you will be regarded as your personal information.

OUR OBLIGATIONS IN TERMS OF APPLICABLE LEGISLATION

HOW DO WE COLLECT YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
Your personal information may be collected while using this website when you contact us when you complete an application form, when you check in at your destination, or when you make use of the services of one of our affiliated business partners.

USE OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
We need to collect your personal information in order to enable you to make use of our website and all products and services offered on our website. We use your personal information in the ordinary course of our business and send you marketing material relating to any other similar sites that we are involved with. We will not disclose your personal information to unrelated third parties or third parties who we do not have business relations with.

PROTECTION OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
Whilst we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information, we take all reasonable steps and measures to keep it secure from loss, misuse, or unauthorized alteration. Our database has built-in safeguards to ensure the privacy and confidentiality of your information. We do not have control over the websites of affiliated parties or sites linked to this site and you must therefore refer to the privacy policies of any such parties to see how they protect your personal information.
